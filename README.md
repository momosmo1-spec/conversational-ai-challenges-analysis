# Conversational AI Challenges Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes major challenges in conversational AI systems, including privacy, bias, reliability, and context management.

The goal is to better understand how conversational AI can be improved to deliver safer, more accurate, and more trustworthy user experiences.

## Key Challenges Covered
- Privacy and data security
- Bias in AI responses
- Context retention and memory limitations
- Response accuracy and hallucinations
- Ethical concerns in AI deployment

## Why This Matters
Conversational AI is becoming part of daily life and business operations. Understanding its limitations is important for building systems that are useful, responsible, and safe.

## Skills Demonstrated
- AI analysis
- Responsible AI thinking
- Risk identification
- Research synthesis
- Technical communication

## Topics Explored
- User trust in AI systems
- Fairness and bias mitigation
- Accuracy and reliability of responses
- Future trends in conversational AI
- Multimodal and context-aware AI systems

## Real-World Application
This project reflects how AI builders, product teams, and organizations must think about safety, privacy, and performance when deploying conversational AI tools.
